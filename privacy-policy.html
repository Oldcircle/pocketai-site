Privacy Policy — PocketAI

Last updated: July 5, 2026
PocketAI has no account system and no servers of its own. It does not collect, transmit to us, or sell any of your data. Your conversations travel directly between your iPhone and your own Mac.

The short version

PocketAI is a client app that lets you use a coding agent running on your own Mac. There is no PocketAI backend and no PocketAI account. We never receive your messages, your files, or any usage data.

What we collect

Nothing. PocketAI has no login, no user profiles, and no server operated by us. We do not receive your prompts, the agent's replies, your file contents, or metadata about your sessions.

How your data moves

  • PocketAI connects to a companion service that you run on your Mac.
  • The connection is made directly to your Mac over an encrypted (TLS) link — typically a secure tunnel that your Mac opens, or your local network. It does not pass through any PocketAI server.
  • Connection details you enter (your Mac's address and an access token) are stored only in the iOS Keychain on your device.
  • Conversation history is stored on your Mac by the companion service, not by us. Deleting a session removes it there.

Analytics & tracking

PocketAI contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We use no cookies, no device identifiers, and no tracking technology. We cannot see how you use the app. A PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy manifest declaring "Data Not Collected" is bundled with the app.

Permissions the app requests

  • Camera — only to scan a pairing QR code to add your Mac. No images are stored or sent.
  • Microphone & Speech Recognition — only for optional voice-to-text in the composer. Transcription uses Apple's on-device recognition where available; PocketAI does not upload your audio.
  • Photos — only when you choose to attach an image to send to your agent. Images go directly to your Mac, not to us.
  • Local Network — only to reach your Mac's companion service when you connect over the same network.

Your agent and model provider

The actual coding agent runs on your Mac and may use a third-party model provider that you have configured (for example, a CLI coding agent and its associated API or subscription). Any prompts, outputs, and billing are governed by that provider's terms and privacy policy, not by PocketAI. PocketAI is not affiliated with, or endorsed by, any such provider.

Children's privacy

PocketAI does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the revised version at this URL.
