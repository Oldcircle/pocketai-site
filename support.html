What PocketAI is

PocketAI lets you use a coding agent that runs on your own Mac from your iPhone or iPad — chat, run tools, review edits, and approve each sensitive step. The agent runs on your computer; PocketAI is the phone client.

Getting started

  1. On your Mac, run the free companion service (one command). It prints a pairing QR code and, if a tunnel is available, a public address so you can connect from anywhere.
  2. In PocketAI, tap + to add your Mac — scan the QR code, or enter the address and token by hand.
  3. Create a session: choose a working folder, model, and permission mode. Start chatting.

No computer handy? Open Try the offline demo from the app to see how a conversation and a permission prompt work.

Frequently asked

Do I need to be on the same Wi-Fi as my Mac?
No. When your Mac opens a secure tunnel, you can connect over cellular or any Wi-Fi. Same-network connections also work.

Does my data go through your servers?
No. There are no PocketAI servers. Your conversation travels directly between your phone and your Mac.

Is there a subscription?
No. PocketAI is a one-time purchase. Any model/agent usage on your Mac is billed by your own provider, not by PocketAI.

Which agents does it work with?
PocketAI is a client for command-line coding agents on your Mac, and works with Claude Code and similar CLI agents.

The connection dropped / I can't connect.
Make sure the companion service is still running on your Mac and that you're using the current address (a quick tunnel's address changes when the service restarts). Re-scan the QR code if needed.
